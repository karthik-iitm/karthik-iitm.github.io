Let $a$ and $b$ be two vectors. Compute the following:

-   $3(a+b)+[(a+b)-(a-b)]$

-   $5(a+b)-[(a+b)-(a-b)]$

-   $3(a+b)+[(a+b)+(a-b)]$

-   $5(a+b)-[(a+b)+(a-b)]$

------------------------------------------------------------------------

First:

$$
\begin{aligned}
3(a+b)+[(a+b)-(a-b)] & =3a+3b+(a+b-a+b)\\
 & =3a+3b+2b\\
 & =3a+5b
\end{aligned}
$$


Second:

$$
\begin{aligned}
5(a+b)-[(a+b)-(a-b)] & =5a+5b-(a+b-a+b)\\
 & =5a+5b-2b\\
 & =5a+3b
\end{aligned}
$$


Third:

$$
\begin{aligned}
3(a+b)+[(a+b)+(a-b)] & =3a+3b+2a\\
 & =5a+3b
\end{aligned}
$$


Fourth:

$$
\begin{aligned}
5(a+b)-[(a+b)+(a-b)] & =5a+5b-2a\\
 & =3a+5b
\end{aligned}
$$
