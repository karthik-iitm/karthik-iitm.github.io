Let $u=(1,1,1)$ and $v=(2,-1,4)$ be two vectors. If $cu+3v=(4,j,k)$, where $c,j,k$ are real numbers, find the value of $c$, $j$ and $k$.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

$$
\begin{aligned}
cu+3v & =(4,j,k)\\
c(1,1,1)+3(2,-1,4) & =(4,j,k)\\
(c+6,c-3,c+12) & =(4,j,k)\\
\implies c & =-2\\
\implies j & =-5\\
\implies k & =10
\end{aligned}
$$
